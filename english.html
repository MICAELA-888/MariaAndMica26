How it all began

We met when we were sixteen, at a school in Sucre. Between nicknames, laughter and afternoons spent together, we discovered love and, along the way, discovered ourselves.

We grew up together, built a family, and learned that even after life’s challenges, we always find our way back to each other.

Today, from Sucre to Buenos Aires, we celebrate the story we continue to choose every day.
